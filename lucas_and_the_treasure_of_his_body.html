Lucas and the treasure of his body

English Version

Title: Lucas and the treasure of his body.

Once upon a time, there was a curious little boy named Lucas who loved discovering new things. One sunny afternoon, while tidying his room, he stumbled upon a colorful book titled "Discovering My Body." Intrigued, Lucas opened it, and thus began an extraordinary adventure.

The first chapter was about the head. Lucas eagerly ran to the mirror and studied his reflection. He touched his hair, wiggled his ears, and scrunched his nose. "Wow," he thought, "my head does so many things!" He realized his eyes helped him see the world, his ears let him hear sounds, and his nose helped him smell delicious cookies baking in the kitchen.

Next, the book guided him to explore his hands and arms. Lucas wiggled his fingers and stretched his arms high above his head. "My hands are like tiny tools," he exclaimed, grasping his favorite toy car. "I can hold, touch, and feel everything around me."

The journey continued to his chest and belly. He placed his hand on his chest and felt the steady thump of his heartbeat. "Thump, thump, thump," it went. "This is my own internal drum that never stops playing," he thought, smiling. When he placed his hand on his belly, he giggled as it rose and fell with each breath.

The book then introduced him to his legs and feet. Lucas stood up and began to jump and run around the room. "My legs take me everywhere," he said joyfully. "And my feet keep me balanced and steady!"

Lastly, Lucas learned about the importance of taking care of his body. The book taught him that he needed to eat healthy foods, drink plenty of water, exercise regularly, and get enough sleep. "My body is like a treasure," he realized, "and I need to take care of it every day."

That night, as Lucas snuggled into bed, he felt excited about all he had discovered. He felt grateful for his wonderful body and promised himself he would always take good care of it. With these thoughts, he drifted off to sleep, dreaming of new adventures and confident that his body was ready to carry him through the wonders of the world.

And so, Lucas lived happily, knowing and caring for his body every single day. The end.

Versión en Español

Título: Lucas y el tesoro de su cuerpo.

Había una vez un niño curioso llamado Lucas, que amaba descubrir cosas nuevas. Una tarde soleada, mientras ordenaba su cuarto, se topó con un libro colorido titulado "Descubriendo mi cuerpo". Intrigado, Lucas lo abrió, y así comenzó una aventura extraordinaria.

El primer capítulo trataba sobre la cabeza. Lucas corrió ansiosamente al espejo y estudió su reflejo. Tocó su cabello, movió sus orejas y arrugó su nariz. "¡Guau!", pensó, "¡mi cabeza hace tantas cosas!" Se dio cuenta de que sus ojos le ayudaban a ver el mundo, sus orejas le permitían escuchar sonidos y su nariz le ayudaba a oler las deliciosas galletas que se horneaban en la cocina.

Luego, el libro lo guió para explorar sus manos y brazos. Lucas movió sus dedos y estiró los brazos por encima de su cabeza. "Mis manos son como pequeñas herramientas", exclamó, agarrando su coche de juguete favorito. "Puedo sostener, tocar y sentir todo a mi alrededor."

La aventura continuó con su pecho y barriga. Colocó su mano en el pecho y sintió el constante latido de su corazón. "Pum, pum, pum", sonaba. "Este es mi propio tambor interno que nunca deja de tocar", pensó, sonriendo. Cuando puso su mano en la barriga, se rió al sentir cómo subía y bajaba con cada respiración.

El libro luego le presentó sus piernas y pies. Lucas se levantó y empezó a saltar y correr por la habitación. "Mis piernas me llevan a todas partes", dijo con alegría. "¡Y mis pies me mantienen equilibrado y firme!"

Por último, Lucas aprendió sobre la importancia de cuidar su cuerpo. El libro le enseñó que debía comer alimentos saludables, beber mucha agua, hacer ejercicio regularmente y dormir lo suficiente. "Mi cuerpo es como un tesoro", se dio cuenta, "¡y debo cuidarlo todos los días!"

Esa noche, mientras Lucas se acurrucaba en la cama, se sintió emocionado por todo lo que había descubierto. Se sintió agradecido por su maravilloso cuerpo y se prometió que siempre lo cuidaría bien. Con estos pensamientos, se quedó dormido, soñando con nuevas aventuras y confiado de que su cuerpo estaba listo para llevarlo a través de las maravillas del mundo.

Y así, Lucas vivió feliz, conociendo y cuidando su cuerpo cada día.
Fin.
